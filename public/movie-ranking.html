The Best Web Series According to Darpan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Mirzapur

"Mirzapur" is a gripping Indian web series that delves into the power struggles, crime, and lawlessness in the eponymous town, highlighting the violent and tumultuous lives of its characters.

The Family Man

"The Family Man" is an engaging Indian web series that follows the life of a middle-class man secretly working as an intelligence officer, balancing the demands of his covert mission with the challenges of his family life.

Chacha Vidhayak Hain Hamare

"Chacha Vidhayak Hain Hamare" is a humorous Indian web series that centers around a young man who falsely claims his uncle is an influential MLA, leading to a series of comedic situations and misunderstandings as he navigates his everyday life.
